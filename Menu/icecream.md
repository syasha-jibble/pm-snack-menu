# 🍦 Ice Cream

The ultimate frozen treat guide—because life is too short for boring flavors.

## Our Favorites

**Classic Vanilla**
A timeless choice. Smooth, creamy, and perfect with just about everything. Don't underestimate the power of perfection.

**Salted Caramel**
Sweet meets salty in this crowd-pleaser. Bonus points if it comes with a drizzle of caramel sauce and pretzel crunch.

**Mint Chocolate Chip**
Refreshing and indulgent at the same time. The perfect flavor to cool you down on a hot day.

**Cookie Dough**
For those who don't believe ice cream without chunks isn't really ice cream. Extra points for the edible cookie dough.

**Strawberry Swirl**
A nostalgic favorite that brings back memories. Fresh, fruity, and pairs beautifully with shortcake.

## Pro Tip
Keep a scoop of each in your freezer—because sometimes one flavor just isn't enough! 🎉

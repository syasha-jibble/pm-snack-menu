# Mocktails

A refreshing selection of non-alcoholic drinks to enjoy with your snacks.

- **Virgin Mojito** - Fresh mint, lime, and sparkling water
- **Mango Sunrise** - Mango puree layered with orange juice and grenadine
- **Berry Fizz** - Mixed berries blended with lemonade and soda
- **Cucumber Cooler** - Cucumber, lime, and a hint of ginger
- **Passion Fruit Spritz** - Passion fruit, elderflower syrup, and tonic water
